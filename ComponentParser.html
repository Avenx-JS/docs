Class: ComponentParser

ComponentParser(styleProcessor)

ComponentParser handles the parsing of Avenx Template (.axt) and Avenx Design (.axd) files. It extracts component state, methods, and templates, and coordinates with the StyleProcessor to handle scoped styles and CSS variables.
